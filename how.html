Why You Need This App

This app helps you with price intelligence. Almost in all cases, the middlemen, you use for spare parts purchases, dont tell you the truth. knowing the truth is a call away,talk to the dealers one-on-one. In case you are a business owner and you have several vehicles, the information this app brings to you is very valuable. You may not have the time to go to Zuba or Apo, this app stands in the gap for you.

How To Use The App

To Search For The Information You Need

You can search for spare dealers, mechanics,and tow van operators. Use the provided search box, enter search keywords, keywords must include alphabets in the information you are looking for. For example, for spare parts dealers search, if you are looking for mercedes spares dealers, type, 'mer' or 'des', for honda type 'hon' or 'da', for toyota type 'toyo' or 'ta'. The above rules apply to search for mechanics also. For tow vans, type the first three letters in the location you are interested in, for example, for berger type 'ber', for gwarimpa type 'gwa', for kubwa type 'kub', for airport road type 'air'.
